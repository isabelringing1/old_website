Vestal
Vestal is a business concept I pitched to investors at the 2016 Power Pitch Competition at
the Illinois Mathematics and Science Academy. The idea was a virtual-reality based social
network where you could interact with others using just a Google Cardboard Viewer and a smartphone.

In addition to a full-length investor pitch, I also had the opportunity to pitch my idea in a matter
of seconds to many people. An aid I used was a quick demo on a Galazy S5 that showed the
capabilities of what Virtual Reality and Vestal could do. A video of the demo is below.
